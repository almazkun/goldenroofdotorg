What are the main stages of the medical treatment trip?

There are 4 main stages:

Estimate and treatment plan - FREE!

Information required for the estimate:
1. First name, Last name and Date of Birth.
2. Scan copies of medical documents and tests with a diagnosis and description of symptoms.
3. Send images of Ultrasound, MRI and CT examinations, if any, to our email: hello@goldenroof.org.

We will send this information to the best hospitals in South Korea.

Budget your trip - FREE!

Addition to cost of treatment, we will provide you with the other costs such as flight, accommodation, transportation, meals.

Prepare you for the travel - FREE!

We take care of everything needed for a trip:
1. Schedule all appointments.
2. Provide you with all necessary documents and visa support.
3. Book your flight and accommodation.
4. Arrange an airport transfer.
